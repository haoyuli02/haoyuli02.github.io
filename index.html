Haoyu Li

I'm a second year CS PhD student at UIUC, where I am fortunate to be adviced by professor Huan Zhang. Previously, I obtained my bachlor's degree in mathematics from UCLA, where I am fortunate to work with professor Cho-Jui Hsieh and professor Yizhou Sun.

I'm broadly interested in machine learning. I have been trying to advance learning based control and their formal verification by enhencing thier scalability to higher-dimensional systems. More recently, I have also been interesting in the reasoning and safety of foundation models.
