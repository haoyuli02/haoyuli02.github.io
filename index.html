Haoyu Li

I'm a second-year CS PhD student at UIUC, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Huan Zhang. Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in mathematics from UCLA, where I am grateful to have worked with Prof. Cho-Jui Hsieh and Prof. Yizhou Sun.

My research spans machine learning, with a focus on learning-based control and its formal verification. More recently, I've also been exploring the reasoning capabilities and safety of foundation models.
